Landscape

Among countless career exploration websites/platforms, two stood out the most and they are Claim Your Future and Roadtrip Nation.

Claim Your Future (CYF) is an educational game that encourages students to explore career pathways, financial decision making, and the future return on investment of post-secondary education and training (Claim Your Future n.d.). According to its developer, the Finance Authority of Maine, CYF began as a classroom game kit, but has since expanded into a digital experience in response to changing trends in education such as remote learning.

Figure 2:

Regarding the online version, after registration, the game presents the player with a few preliminary questions to test his/her general knowledge, after which the player is greeted with the main dashboard (See figure 2) where he/she can plan out his/her career path, budget, housing option, and so on. Important information such as salary, student loans, and education are pulled from the web (it should be noted that this information is catered to US audiences only).

Figure 3:

At the core of Roadtrip Nation are videos of interviews with inspirational individuals who have managed to carve out their own path in life with the main goal to open our eyes to what a fulfilling career can truly be. The site also features a career exploration tool where students and career seekers only need to answer three questions and the site will automatically pull content e.g. interviews, jobs, leaders in that field for him/her to learn more about the role (Figure 3)

What separates our project from CYF and Roadtrip Nation is the absence of value-added components such as providing a budget estimation or introducing the players to successful people within their chosen field to further inspire them.
