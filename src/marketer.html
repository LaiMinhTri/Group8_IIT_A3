Marketing and sales professional

Almost every project requires the hiring of a marketing or sales professional. The success of a project is contingent upon public awareness and participation, as well as the provision of constructive feedback on the project's progress. Additionally, we need to promote our game to ensure that businesses are aware of our existence and, hopefully, willing to contribute to the funding of our project. We are looking for individuals who are visionary and creative in their thinking, as well as individuals with prior marketing experience and knowledge, to fill this position. This task is necessary because our game requires a significant amount of information from the user to function properly.

Responsibility:

  • Developing new products and promoting established brands for the company
  • Budget planning and analysis for the coming year, as well as developing and implementing marketing strategies and plans based on market research.
  • Producing reports by collecting, analysing, and summarising market data.
  • Developing new public relations and marketing campaigns.
