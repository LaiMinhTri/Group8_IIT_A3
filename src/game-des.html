The Game Designer (GD) would be responsible for creating the gameâ€™s environment, assets, character models, gameplay system, and user experience of our game. He/she would ensure that the mood, look, and feel of the game align with our initial ideas. The GD needs to work closely with the Game Developer to code and load all of his/her design into a finished product.

Responsibility:

  • Consider, plan and detail every element of a new game including the setting, rules, story flow, props, vehicles, character interface and modes of play
  • Conduct market research to understand what your target audience wants
  • Write scripts and design storyboards
  • Work collaboratively with others, including game developers, artists and programmers, to produce a prototype
