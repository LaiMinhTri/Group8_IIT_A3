Skills and Jobs

Game developer

To accomplish the goal of creating a fully playable game, this project will require the assistance of a game developer. We will be looking for someone with prior experience with Gamemaker studio 2 and the Gamemaker language, as this is the game engine our team chose and has been working with for several weeks, as well as strong communication and collaboration skills. As an added benefit, given our inexperience with coding and game production, we may benefit from the assistance of someone with extensive experience in the field to assist us in developing new ideas and improving existing ones. Due to the complexity and significance of the game, this position is critical to the project's success.

Responsibilities:

  • Converting design concepts into usable game code
  • Using Gamemaker studio 2 to develop responsive 3D applications for PC or mobile
  • Prototyping gameplay concepts and features
  • Conducting research and implementing other game engine-compatible technologies, such as augmented reality, virtual reality, and augmented reality mixed reality
  • Troubleshooting and bug correction
  • Contribute to technical documents by providing code documentation and other inputs

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or Software Engineering.
  • Experiential knowledge of the entire game design process.
  • Programming languages expertise, including C++, Java, and C.
  • Extensive experience developing libraries and APIs.
  • Current knowledge of gaming trends.
  • Excellent artistic and technical abilities.
  • Capability to work in a team environment.
  • Capacity to troubleshoot and resolve complex software and coding issues.

Game Designer

The Game Designer (GD) would be responsible for creating the game’s environment, assets, character models, gameplay system, and user experience of our game. He/she would ensure that the mood, look, and feel of the game align with our initial ideas. The GD needs to work closely with the Game Developer to code and load all of his/her design into a finished product.

Responsibilities:

  • Write scripts and design storyboards
  • Conduct market research to understand what your target audience wants
  • Consider, plan and detail every element of a new game including the setting, rules, story flow, props, vehicles, character interface and modes of play
  • Work collaboratively with others, including game developers, artists and programmers, to produce a prototype

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in game design, computer science or computer engineering.
  • At least 2-3 years of experience in the relevant position.
  • Enthusiasm and knowledge of the industry.
  • Knowledge of Python, C++ programming languages is an advantage.
  • Experience with game engines such as Unreal Engine.
  • Portfolio of sample projects.

A person who has a broad knowledge of different industries

Since the purpose of this game is to give the young generation a deeper understanding about different careers, we need to employ somebody that has experience in many working fields, especially Art and Design, IT and Business industries. This person will integrate his rich and varied experience of life into the game story to educate the youngster. He/she will also take charge in creating tasks and giving solutions to the problems. This position is crucial to our project because if we generate the wrong knowledge about jobs, it will reduce our image and reputation. Besides that, an individual who has an impressively in-depth knowledge can help our team to develop the game if we want to expand more industries in the future. Because this is an educational game, it will save a lot of time and resources if our team has a worldly-wise person.

Responsibilities:

  • Create tasks and give solutions for each industry
  • Analyse the game market and the opponents to come up with new idea for the expansion of new industries
  • Write scripts and design storyboards
  • Collaborate with other departments to fix the problems related to game story and tasks after releasing the game

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree of indigenous knowledge systems careers or other relevant fields
  • At least 5-8 years of experience in the relevant position.
  • Enthusiasm and knowledge of different industries.
  • Knowledge of programming languages (GML, HTML, CSS, JavaScript) is an advantage.

Marketing and sales professional

Almost every project requires the hiring of a marketing or sales professional. The success of a project is contingent upon public awareness and participation, as well as the provision of constructive feedback on the project's progress. Additionally, we need to promote our game to ensure that businesses are aware of our existence and, hopefully, willing to contribute to the funding of our project. We are looking for individuals who are visionary and creative in their thinking, as well as individuals with prior marketing experience and knowledge, to fill this position. This task is necessary because our game requires a significant amount of information from the user to function properly.

Responsibilities:

  • Developing new products and promoting established brands for the company
  • Budget planning and analysis for the coming year, as well as developing and implementing marketing strategies and plans based on market research.
  • Producing reports by collecting, analysing, and summarising market data.
  • Developing new public relations and marketing campaigns.

Requirements:

  • A bachelor's degree in marketing, mathematics, business administration, or a closely related field is required.
  • 3 to 5 years of marketing or sales experience
  • It may be advantageous to have management experience.
  • Sales and marketing comprehension and knowledge
  • Excellent analytical, organizational, and critical thinking abilities.
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, as well as a strong commitment to customer service.
  • The ability to analyze data and write reports.
  • Capacity for comprehending and adhering to corporate policies and procedures.
  • Capacity to perform well under duress.
